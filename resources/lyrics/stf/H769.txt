---[Verse:1]---

All glory to the Father be,
the Spirit and the Son:
all glory to the One in Three
while endless ages run.
Alleluia! Amen.


St Fulbert of Chartres (d. 1028)
translated byRobert Campbell (1814-1868)
as found in The Methodist Worship Book
