Neither death, nor life,
nor angels, nor powers,
nor trials in the present,
nor any trial to come,
neither height, nor depth,
nor all of creation
can ever separate us from the love of God
poured out in Christ Jesus, Christ Jesus our Lord.


Marty Haugen (b. 1950)
