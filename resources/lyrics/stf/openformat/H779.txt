v1 c1 v2 c1 v3 c1

Holy, holy, holy.
With joy my heart adores you!
My heart cries out to worship you:
holy are you, Lord!

Original Spanish:
Santo, santo, santo.
!Mi corazon te adora!
!Mi corazon te sabe decir:
!santo eres Senor!


Argentinian liturgical text
