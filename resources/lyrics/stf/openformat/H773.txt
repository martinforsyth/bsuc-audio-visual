Praise the Lover of Creation,
praise the Spirit, Friend of Friends,
praise the true Beloved, our Saviour,
praise the God who makes and mends,
strong, surrendered, many-splendoured,
Three whose Oneness never ends.


Brian Wren (b. 1936)
