



1

We sing of your glory,
we praise you again,
for you are eternal. Amen. Amen.

2

We sing of your power
and honour again,
for you are eternal. Amen. Amen.

3

We sing of surrender
to you, God, again.
Your power is eternal. Amen. Amen.

4

'Glory in the highest,
on earth,' sing again.
Glory, alleluia. Amen. Amen.
