Great is he who's the King of kings
and the Lord of lords,
he is wonderful!

Alleluia, alleluia,
alleluia, he is wonderful!

Alleluia, salvation and glory,
honour and power, he is wonderful!


Anonymous

This song can be sung as a two- or three-part round.
