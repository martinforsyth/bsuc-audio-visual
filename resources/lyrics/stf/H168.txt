---[Verse:1]---

Come, Lord Jesus, come.
Come, Lord Jesus, come.
Come, Lord Jesus, come,
to this world of ours.

---[Verse:2]---

Lord, we need you now;
Lord, we need you now;
Lord, we need you now
in this world of ours.

---[Verse:3]---

Fill us with your peace;
fill us with your peace;
fill us with your peace
in this world of ours.

---[Verse:4]---

Touch us with your love;
touch us with your love;
touch us with your love
in this world of ours.

---[Verse:5]---

May we see your light;
may we see your light;
may we see your light
in this world of ours.


Francesca Leftley (b. 1955)
