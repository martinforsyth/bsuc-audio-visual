---[Chorus:1]---
v1 c1 v2 c1 v3 c1

1

To be in your presence,
to sit at your feet,
where your love surrounds me,
and makes me complete:
This is my desire, O Lord,
this is my desire.
This is my desire, O Lord,
this is my desire.

2

To rest in your presence,
not rushing away,
to cherish each moment --
here I would stay.


Noel Richards (b. 1955)
